# User Guide

## Resetting your password

Users can reset their password from the Account Settings page.
Navigate to Settings, select the Security tab, and click Reset Password.
If you cannot access your account, click Forgot Password on the login
page, enter your registered email address, and follow the reset link
sent to your inbox. The reset link expires after 24 hours.

## Uploading documents

Users can upload documents from the Documents page. Supported file
types include PDF and TXT. The maximum file size is 20MB per upload.
After upload, the system will process the document and make it
available for search within a few minutes.

## Inviting team members

Admins can invite team members from the Team Settings page. Enter the
user's email address and select the appropriate role. The invited user
will receive an email with instructions to set up their account.
Pending invitations can be cancelled from the same page.

## Changing your account email

To change your account email, go to Account Settings and select
Profile. Enter your new email address and confirm with your current
password. A verification link will be sent to the new email address
before the change takes effect.
