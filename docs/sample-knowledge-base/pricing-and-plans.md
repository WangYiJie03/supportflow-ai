# Pricing and Plans

## Free plan

The Free plan is designed for individual users who want to explore
the platform. It includes up to 3 document uploads, limited AI
queries per month, and community support.

## Pro plan

The Pro plan is designed for small teams. It includes up to 50
document uploads, unlimited AI queries, priority email support, and
team collaboration features. Billing is monthly or annual.

## Enterprise plan

The Enterprise plan is designed for organisations that need advanced
security, custom integrations, dedicated onboarding support, and a
dedicated account manager. Enterprise plans are custom-priced based
on usage and team size.

Specific enterprise pricing is not available in the public knowledge
base because pricing depends on usage, team size, and custom
requirements. Customers who ask about enterprise pricing should be
escalated to the sales or support team for human review.

For enterprise pricing, customers should contact the sales team
directly through the contact form on the website.

## Changing your plan

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the Billing
section in Account Settings. Changes take effect at the start of the
next billing cycle.
