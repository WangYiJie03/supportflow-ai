# Refund Policy

## Refund window

Customers on the Pro plan can request a full refund within 14 days
of the initial purchase if they have not exceeded fair usage limits.
Annual plan refunds are prorated based on unused months.

## Non-refundable cases

Refunds are not available for custom enterprise implementation work,
completed onboarding sessions, or accounts found to have violated
the terms of service.

## How to request a refund

To request a refund, contact the support team with your account
email address, payment receipt, and reason for the request. Refund
requests are reviewed within 3 business days.
